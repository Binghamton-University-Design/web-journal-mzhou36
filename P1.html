For Project 1, we need to create a very deliberate visual language that attempts to communicate something about myself and the world I live in.

Things that we are sharing in our world is the celebration of the various festival. In those festivals, we are sharing the same culture and emotions. Since we do not know whom we communicate with, my idea is to display the distinct elements in those festivals. I use the clock to represent Months since they all have 12 parts and all represent time.
